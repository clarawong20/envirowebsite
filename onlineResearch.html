Online Resources you can use to Help

There are many everyday resources that people can use to help the environment.

  • Ecosia → Plant trees while searching the web.
  • David Suzuki → plenty of Canada-based petitions to check out and sign!
  • NASA's Images of Change → provides a stark comparison of how our world is changing due to climate change, urbanization, and increased natural disasters.
  • How to sort your trash → Peel resource, enter your trash item and it’ll tell you what bin it belongs to
  • Eco Footprint → test your ecological footprint
