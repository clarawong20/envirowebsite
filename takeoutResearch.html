Problems with cutlery and takeouts

Fast food has quickly become one of the easier options when it comes to food. In 2018, an estimated 2025 million takeout containers were used in the European Union alone. According to the US Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), food and food packaging materials make up almost half of all municipal solid waste. Most plastic today derives from natural gas and petroleum. While many companies have made the switch to sustainable alternatives, words like “compostable” and “biodegradable” are not regulated. Different types of bioplastics compost in different ways. While the name indicates that they decompose quickly and safely in nature, these terms actually just indicate that they are made from natural raw materials like maize, cane sugar, or potatoes rather than oil. Many of which have similar properties to regular plastic.
