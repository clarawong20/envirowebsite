Problems with Plastic Bags

When it comes to shopping, plastic bags are most commonly used. It is estimated that Americans use around 100 billion plastic bags a year. Plastic bags can take up to 1000 years to degrade. When they end up in landfills, plastic bags slowly release harmful chemicals.The wind usually picks up the plastic bags from landfills and drops them off in oceans, where wildlife mistakes them as food and chokes on the plastic bags.
