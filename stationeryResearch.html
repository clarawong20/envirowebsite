Problems with Stationeries

Paper accounts for around 26% of total waste at landfills. Producing paper notebooks creates deforestation, contributes to air pollution and uses large amounts of energy. The plastic used as a cover for notebooks goes to waste and takes a very long time to decompose. Not to mention it goes through resources very quickly.

Solutions

Paper Products

  • Ecojot → This Canadian company manufactures paper products that are 100% recycled paper and use vegetable inks and adhesives to put it all together.
  • Rocketbook → A digital and reusble notebook that is eco-friendly.
  • PooPooPaper → This company makes cards, notebooks, crafts and more from manure of herbivorous animals.
  • Green Field Paper Company → This American company creates sustainable paper products (invitations, pacckaging, etc.).
